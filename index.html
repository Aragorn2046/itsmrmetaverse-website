For over two decades, I've been tracking the collision between exponential technology and human society. As a Future Historian, I don't predict the future. I map its patterns, trace its signals, and help organizations see what's already emerging before their competitors do.

From the first days of commercial VR to the rise of generative AI, I've been in the room where the next wave was building. I've advised boardrooms, challenged conferences, and helped leaders rethink their assumptions about what's coming and how fast it's arriving.

My work sits at the intersection of AI, immersive technology, and exponential disruption. I translate complexity into clarity, and urgency into action.
